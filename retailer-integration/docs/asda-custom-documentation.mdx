## Beacon Description

Beacons that are returned once per format

<table>
 <thead>
  <tr>
   <th>
    <p>
     Beacon key
    </p>
   </th>
   <th>
    <p>
     Description
    </p>
   </th>
  </tr>
 </thead>
 <tbody>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onLoadBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Indicates that the ad has been rendered (even if not viewable yet)
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onViewBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Indicates that the ad has been viewed (viewed = 50% of the ad in viewport for 1 second)
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     OnFileClickBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Indicates that a click was made on the file option
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     OnBundleBasketChangeBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     add multiple products at ones
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
 </tbody>
</table>

Beacons that are returned once per product:

<table>
 <thead>
  <tr>
   <th>
   </th>
   <th>
   </th>
  </tr>
 </thead>
 <tbody>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onLoadBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Indicates that the product tile was rendered (even if not viewable yet)
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onViewBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Indicates that the ad has been viewed (viewed = 50% of the ad in viewport for 1 second)
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     ProductPage
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Clicktracking that redirects to the product detail page
    </p>
    <p>
     Should be fired every time for every single click
    </p>
    <p>
     (only applicable for web environment)
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onClickBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Clicktracking without redirection.
    </p>
    <p>
     Should be used for clicks in case ProductPage is not a viable option. (e.g. InApp environment, AddtoCart)
    </p>
    <p>
     Should be fired every time for every single click
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onBasketChangeBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Added/removed quantity needs to be added as query string parameter (optional, defaults to 1)
    </p>
    <p>
     Example: \&amp;quantity=2 or \&amp;quantity=-1
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
   <td>
    <p>
     onWishlistBeacon
    </p>
   </td>
   <td>
    <p>
     Indicates that a click was made on the Add to Wishlist button on the product tile
    </p>
   </td>
  </tr>
 </tbody>
</table>
