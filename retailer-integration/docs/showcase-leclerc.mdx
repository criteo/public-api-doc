## Desktop Specification

![](https://files.readme.io/b8fd247-Showcase_fields.jpg "Showcase_fields.jpg")

### General

![](https://files.readme.io/19e2447-SC_full_size.jpg "SC_full size.jpg")

* The Showcase solution is composed of 2 parts: the **branding zone** on the left side, and the **product zone** on the right side.
* The format must be able to host at least 1 product to 3 products maximum.
* The branding zone size is equal to one product tile size.
  * If the branding zone is smaller than the background image, then the image is cropped on both sides uniformly and/or at the bottom.
  * If the branding zone is bigger than the background image, the extra available space is filled with a plain colored background (CSS).
* The product zone design and layout are the same as the other product tiles.
* The solution may have a custom title above.

### Available Options

> On the options area (only one can be applicable simultaneously for this format) 

* Redirection (redirection link)
  * At the button's click, it redirects to the link defined on the platform. It's possible to have it on *Self* and *Blank* redirection options (not applicable for App)
* Shop (redirection)
  * At the button's click, it redirects to the link defined on the platform. It's possible to have it on *Self* and *Blank* redirection options (not applicable for App). The user will insert a text to correspond a new landing page with all product id present in the line item. It's a specific redirection 
* Legal
  * Editable text field.

![](https://files.readme.io/7d49589-Showcase_Options.jpg "Showcase_Options.jpg")

### Technical details

* There is a safe zone at the top of the format which is 180x320px for the 310x500px creative and 180x190px for the 270x390px creative. This area should remain visible for every breakpoint.
* CTA Button
  * Button size: 150x30px.
  * Button font: Roboto Regular, 12px. 
* Legal
  * Space size occupied by legal depends on the tile width. There is 10px padding on both the right and the left side of the tile.
  * Legal font: Roboto Regular, 10px.
  * The gap between the options area and the bottom of the branding zone is fixed to 7px.
  * The options area height is fixed at 30px.

![](https://files.readme.io/d73b9e6-Showcase_sizes.jpg "Showcase_sizes.jpg")

* There is a 1px fixed border around branding and product zones.

### Assets sizes:

* Background image normal: 270x390px (with safe zone fixed: 180x190px) 
* OR Background image big: 310x500px (with safe zone fixed: 180x320px)

# Tracking ad activity

Ad activity is tracked through beacons. Those are GET HTTPS calls on the client side that need to be triggered both when a set of standard actions take place and when specific actions take place 

The list of available beacons will depend on the format of the ad.  

Some beacons are tied to the whole ad, while others are tied to specific products. Note that any user interaction with the ad should only trigger one call.\
If the user repeats the same action multiple times (for example, by increasing the quantity of a given product in his basket), the corresponding beacon needs to be triggered the same number of times.
