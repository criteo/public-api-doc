* It's composed of two different parts: **branding zone** on the left side and **product zone** on the right side.

* The user can change the product displayed in the product zone with product buttons.
* The branding zone size is equal to the product tile size. When a website is responsive, both tiles must always be at the same size. 
  * If the branding zone is smaller than the background image, then the image is cropped on both sides uniformly and/or at the bottom.
  * If the branding zone is bigger than the background image, the extra available space is filled with a plain colored background (CSS). 
* The product zone design and layout are the same as the other product tiles. 

### Available Options:

In the options area (only one option is available at the same time per format). 

* Redirection (redirection link)
  * At the button's click, it redirects to the link defined on the platform. It's possible to have it on *Self* and *Blank* redirection options (not applicable for App)
* Shop (redirection to a "boutique")
  * At the button's click, it redirects to the link defined on the platform. It's possible to have it on *Self* and *Blank* redirection options (not applicable for App). In the landing page we find two assets for a banner (desktop and mobile), a title and all information that concerns the products (title, price, beacon...) 
* Legal
  * Editable text field.

### Technical Details:

* There is a safe zone at the top of the format which is 180 x 140 px. This area should remain visible for every breakpoint. 
* It's possible to have up to 5 product buttons and 1 option: a call to action (CTA) button or legal text. The number of product buttons depends on the size of the branding zone. 
* Product Buttons
  * Button size: 150 x 30 px.
  * Button font: Roboto Regular, 12 px.
  * There can be two colors for background product buttons: one for the hover state (selected) and another when not selected.  
  * There can be two colors for text product buttons: one for the hover state (selected) and another when not selected. 
  * The text can be over two lines and, in this case, has a line height of 1. 
* CTA Button
  * Button size: 150 x 30 px.
  * Button font: Roboto Regular, 12 px. 
* Legal
  * The space size occupied by legal text depends on the tile width. There is 10 px padding on the right and the left of the tile.
  * Legal font: Roboto Regular, 10 px.
  * The gap between the options area and the bottom of the branding zone is fixed to 7 px.
  * The options area height is fixed at 30 px.

# Tracking Ad Activity

Ad activity is tracked through beacons. These are GET HTTPS calls on the client side that need to be triggered both when a set of standard actions take place and when specific actions take place. 

The list of available beacons depends on the format of the ad.  

Some beacons are tied to the whole ad, while others are tied to specific products. Note that any user interaction with the ad should only trigger one call.\
If the user repeats the same action multiple times (for example, increasing the quantity of a given product in his basket), the corresponding beacon needs to be triggered the same number of times.
