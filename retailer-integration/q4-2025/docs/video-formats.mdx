This section presents the two video-based ad formats available for integration in Retail Media:

* **Commerce Video Spotlight (CVS)**
* **Branding Video Standout (BVS)**
* **Commerce Video Grid Duet (CVGD)**

Both leverage **VAST 4.2** for video delivery, support desktop and mobile layouts, and include configurable rendering keys for creative consistency across placements.

***

## Commerce Video Spotlight (CVS)

CVS is a **commerce-focused format** combining a video and a shoppable product zone. It is designed to bridge **branding impact and product engagement**, it adapts responsively and supports optional interaction and styling parameters (e.g., legal text, color mode, redirect behavior).

***

## Branding Video Standout (BVS)

BVS is a **storytelling-oriented format** that merges brand video, image, and message elements into a unified layout. It supports CTA interaction, consistent redirect logic, and a modular design that aligns with both awareness and commerce goals.

***

## Commerce Video Grid Duet (CVGD)

CVGD is a **commerce-focused video format** that pairs a vertical branding video with a scrollable product zone. It supports a CTA redirection and responsive design for consistent performance across devices.
