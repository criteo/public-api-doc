This article will explain how to edit your organization's information.

1. From the navigation, select Settings

![1650](/images/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/9e4c227-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201032.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201032.png")

2. Click the pencil logo to edit your current organization information and save your changes

![1873](/images/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/41a0a18-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201104.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201104.png")

3. If you want to change another organization's information a. Go back to the My Apps screen b. Select the Organization in the Organization screen accessible at the top right corner

![461](/images/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/52272fd-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201338.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201338.png")

c. Change the Organization

![1425](/images/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/9f12117-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201418.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201418.png")

d. Follow steps 1 and 2

If you have any question about the required fields, see the [Create your organization](doc:create-your-organization) guide.
