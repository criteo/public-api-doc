## **Introduction**

The Statistics endpoint helps you merge your Criteo data with other sources, build business alerting on Criteo data, and programmatically manage your ad spend based on campaign performance.  

The Statistics API allows you to measure and report on your advertising campaigns, through dimensions and metrics that you specify.
