This article will help you to create your Criteo developer account that will give you access to Developer Portal as well as to the other Criteo platforms. If you have already a Criteo account (for example created through Management Center, Retail Media Platform or PMC) you can skip this article.

1. Go to our landing page at our [Criteo Developer Portal](/) and click on Get Started or go directly to our [Criteo Developer Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/)
2. Complete our registration form to create a Criteo account.
3. Validate the confirmation email to finish the process.
4. You will be redirected to the [Criteo Developer Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/). If the redirection fails please click on the Developer Portal link in your account screen.

If you want to modify your account please see our article [Managing my developer account](/marketing-solutions/v2020.07/docs/managing-my-developer-account)
