This article will explain you how to edit your organization information.

1. Click on the top left corner menu and select Settings

![1650](/images/marketing-solutions/v2020.07/docs/9e4c227-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201032.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201032.png")

2. Click on the pencil logo to Edit your current Organization information and save your changes

![1873](/images/marketing-solutions/v2020.07/docs/41a0a18-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201104.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201104.png")

3. If you want to change another Organization information

   1. Go back to the My Apps screen
   2. Select the Organization in the Organization screen accessible at the top right corner

      ![461](/images/marketing-solutions/v2020.07/docs/52272fd-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201338.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201338.png")

   3. Change the Organization

      ![1425](/images/marketing-solutions/v2020.07/docs/9f12117-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201418.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201418.png")

   4. Follow steps 1 and 2

If you have any question about required fields please see the [Create your organization](/marketing-solutions/v2020.07/docs/create-your-organization) guide.
