This article will help you to access your Criteo partner account. This will give you access to Partner Portal, as well as to the other Criteo platforms.

1. Go to our landing page at our [Criteo Partner Portal](https://developers.criteo.com/) and click on Get Started or go directly to our [Criteo Partner Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/)

<Info>
  **Criteo Login Required**

  Before creating your API application, you’ll need to log in to the Criteo Partner Portal using your **Criteo credentials**. If you don’t have credentials, please contact your Criteo account representative for access.
</Info>

1. Login with your existing Criteo account or contact your Criteo representative to create an account.
2. Once logged in, you will be redirected to the [Criteo Partner Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/). Please click on the Developer Portal link on your account screen if the redirection fails.

If you want to modify your account, please see our article [Managing my partner account](/marketing-solutions/v2025.07/docs/managing-my-developer-account)
