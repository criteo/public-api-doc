This article will explain how to edit your organization's information.

1. From the navigation, select Settings

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/marketing-solutions/v2025.07/docs/9e4c227-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201032.png" />
</Frame>

2. Click the pencil logo to edit your current organization information and save your changes

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/marketing-solutions/v2025.07/docs/41a0a18-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201104.png" />
</Frame>

3. If you want to change another organization's information

   1. Go back to the My Apps screen
   2. Select the Organization in the Organization screen accessible at the top right corner

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/marketing-solutions/v2025.07/docs/52272fd-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201338.png" />
      </Frame>

   3. Change the Organization

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/marketing-solutions/v2025.07/docs/9f12117-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201418.png" />
      </Frame>

   4. Follow steps 1 and 2

If you have any question about the required fields, see the [Create your organization](/marketing-solutions/v2025.07/docs/create-your-organization) guide.
