Need a hand? Our developer resources are here for you:

**[FAQ](doc:migration-faq)** Find the answers to many common questions about our API, including migrating from our legacy APIs.

Can't find what you need in the documentation? Get in touch with your Criteo contact.  

 

## Get involved in shaping our API.

Join our developer program and shape the future of the Criteo API. Whether you're working on an app just for you or for many advertisers, your insights will help us power the next wave of advertising.

The developer program is here for you to get more value out of your integrations. Get the support you need, connect with our developer community, and share your feedback directly with our product team.

* Get early access to participate in exclusive betas
* Be the first to know about new features and updates
* Speak with our product team about your API needs and feedback
* Join our community of experts and connect with fellow developers

Help us make our next release the best one yet. To join, email us at [\[email protected\]](https://www.criteo.com/contact/) or get in touch with your Criteo contact.
