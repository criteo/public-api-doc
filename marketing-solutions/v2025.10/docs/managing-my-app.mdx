This section will explain how to edit your app information (before publishing your app).

1. Select an application you have previously created in your My Apps space

![1853](https://files.readme.io/e717a51-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_202059.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 202059.png")

2. Click on edit next to the app name

![1857](https://files.readme.io/51503fa-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_202203.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 202203.png")

3. Edit the app name, description, or image (this is only possible before publishing an app)
