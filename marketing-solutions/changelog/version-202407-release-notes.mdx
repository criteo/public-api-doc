We are pleased to announce the release of Version 2024.07 of the Criteo API. This is a stable API version that will be fully supported until August 2025. Please ensure you review the updates and adjust your integrations accordingly. As always, our updated client libraries (Python, Java, and PHP) and Postman collections are available to help you get started.

## What's New

Several endpoints have transitioned from the preview stage to the stable version, ensuring improved stability and reliability for your API interactions. These endpoints focus on enhancing the management and retrieval of Ad Sets:

* **GET** `/marketing-solutions/ad-sets/{ad-set-id}`
* **POST** `/marketing-solutions/ad-sets/search`
* **POST** `/marketing-solutions/ad-sets`
* **PATCH** `/marketing-solutions/ad-sets`

These endpoints are now fully supported and ready for production use. They provide greater flexibility and control over ad set management, ensuring that your marketing campaigns are more efficiently handled.

## What's Changed

As part of our ongoing efforts to streamline and modernize our API, we have decommissioned several legacy endpoints. These endpoints have been deprecated and are no longer supported, effective immediately:

### Legacy Audience APIs (Removed)

* **GET** `/audiences`
* **POST** `/audiences`
* **DELETE** `/audiences/{audience-id}`
* **PATCH** `/audiences/{audience-id}`
* **DELETE** `/audiences/{audience-id}/contactlist`
* **PATCH** `/audiences/{audience-id}/contactlist`

### Contact List API (Removed)

* **GET** `/marketing-solutions/audience-segments/{audience-segment-id}/contact-list`

For further details regarding these decommissioned endpoints, please consult the respective legacy documentation pages:

* Legacy Audience Removed: [https://developers.criteo.com/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/audience-legacy](/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/audience-legacy)
* Contact List Removed: [https://developers.criteo.com/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/contact-list](/marketing-solutions/v2023.07/docs/contact-list)

We recommend transitioning to the latest endpoints and integrating these changes into your workflows. For additional support, please refer to our updated documentation or reach out to our support team.

Thank you for your continued partnership and commitment to using the Criteo API.
