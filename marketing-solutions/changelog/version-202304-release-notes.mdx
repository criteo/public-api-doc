Version 2023.04 of the Criteo API is live as of April 24th, 2023. This version will be supported until April 30th, 2024.

While this update does not introduce any changes or new features, it is important to maintain transparent communication with our valued users. Rest assured that our team has been working diligently behind the scenes to ensure the stability and reliability of our API.

In this release, we have focused on various under-the-hood enhancements, bug fixes, and performance optimizations. Our primary goal is continuously improving the overall user experience and ensuring the API remains robust and dependable.

We understand that even incremental updates are essential for you to stay informed about the progress of our API. We value your feedback and are committed to continually enhancing our services based on your needs and expectations.

Thank you for being a part of our API community!
