<Info>
  The Criteo Retail Media API helps you unlock a range of possibilities to help you enhance your media performance from any platform. Our suite of tools empowers you to seamlessly create, launch, and monitor your campaigns, providing a comprehensive view of your performance.
</Info>

Our APIs have been instrumental in helping our marketing partners, agencies, brands and retailers grow their marketing business.

## Campaign Management

Campaign managed endpoints are designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients and partners.  
Our dynamic Retail Media API allows you to manage and fine-tune all your campaigns with high precision.

<Columns cols={4}>
  <Card title="Campaign Management" icon="fa-bullseye">
    Create, update, and monitor retail media campaigns and line items through a unified API.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Configuration Control" icon="fa-sliders-h">
    Manage budgets, attribution models, and balances programmatically for full setup flexibility.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Line Item Controls" icon="fa-tachometer-alt">
    Adjust bids, pacing, and flight dates directly via API endpoints.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Product & Keyword Targeting" icon="fa-tags">

  </Card>
