---
title: "Create Your Partner Account"
---

This article will help you access your **Criteo Partner account**, giving you entry to the Partner Portal and other Criteo platforms.

<Steps>
    <Step>
        To begin, log in via the [Criteo Partner Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/),

        <Info> 
        **Criteo Login Required**
        Before creating your API application, you’ll need to log in to the Criteo Partner Portal using your **Criteo credentials**. If you don’t have credentials, please contact your Criteo account representative for access.
        </Info> 
    </Step>

    <Step>
        Login with your existing Criteo account,
    </Step>
    <Step>
        After logging in, you will be redirected to the [Criteo Partner Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/). If the redirection fails, simply click on the Partner Portal link from your account screen.
    </Step>
</Steps>
