If you need to update your account information, you can do so by logging into the Criteo API partner portal. This guide will walk you through the steps to edit your account details.

1. Click the user icon in the top right corner of the page to access your account information.

<Frame>
![](/images/retail-media/v2024.10/docs/ab56229-image.png)
</Frame>

2. You can edit your first name, last name, and display name on this page. If needed, you can also change your password.

<Info>
  To change your password, you will be required to provide your current password for verification.
</Info>

<Frame caption="Managing user's contact information in the Criteo partner portal.">
  <img src="/images/retail-media/v2024.10/docs/70409c7-image.png" />
</Frame>

***

## Request to delete an account

If you wish to delete your Criteo account because you no longer work with Criteo, please contact us by sending an email to [support@criteo.com](mailto:support@criteo.com).
