This article will help you access your Criteo developer account, giving you entry to the Developer Portal and other Criteo platforms.

1. To begin, log in via the [Criteo Developer Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/),

<Info>
  ### Criteo Login Required

  Before creating your API application, you’ll need to log in to the Criteo Developer Portal using your **Criteo credentials**. If you don’t have credentials, contact your Criteo account representative for access.
</Info>

2. Login with your existing Criteo account,
3. After logging in, you will be redirected to the [Criteo Developer Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/). If the redirection fails, simply click on the Developer Portal link from your account screen.
