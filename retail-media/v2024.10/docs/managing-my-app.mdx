This section explains how to edit your app information before publication.

1. Select an application you have previously created from your `My Apps` space.

![](/images/retail-media/v2024.10/docs/423bba6-myapp1.png "myapp1.png")

2. Click "Edit" on the right side of the app name.

![](/images/retail-media/v2024.10/docs/6f9205b-myapp2.png "myapp2.png")

3. You can update the app name, description, or image. Note that these changes are only possible before the app is published.

![](/images/retail-media/v2024.10/docs/de51863-app3.png "app3.png")
