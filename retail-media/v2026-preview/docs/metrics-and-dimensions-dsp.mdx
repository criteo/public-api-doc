## **Report Metrics**

* Most metrics are available for every report type, with some exceptions to `assistedSales` and `assistedUnits` which apply only to Line Item reports

* The attributed Transactions report only has two metrics: `attributedSales` and `attributedUnits` 

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Metric</th>
      <th>Report Level</th>
      <th>Report Types</th>
      <th>Description</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td><code>impressions</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td><ul><li><strong>Report Types:</strong> Campaign reports</li></ul><br />Measured when an ad creative renders on a page; an ad creative may display products from one or more campaigns; each unique campaign is counted once; used in all report types except for Product and Product Category reports<br /><ul><li><strong>Report Types:</strong> Line Item reports</li></ul><br />Measured when an ad creative renders on a page; an ad creative may display products from one or more line items; each unique line item is counted once; used in all report types except for Product and Product Category reports<br /><ul><li><strong>Report Types: </strong>Product and Product Category</li></ul><br />Measured when a promoted product is rendered on the page, whether as a single ad creative or as one of many products in an ad creative; each product is counted once separately</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>clicks</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Measured when an ad creative is clicked on, which incurs a cost to the advertiser for open auction campaigns</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>spend</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>For open auctions campaigns, the total cost of clicks for the advertiser, inclusive of platform fees</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>attributedSales</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product-category, product, attributedTransactions</td>
      <td>Sales revenue attributed to a click or impression, exclusive of <code>assistedSales</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>attributedUnits</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product, attributedTransactions</td>
      <td>Units sold attributed to a click or impression, exclusive of.<code>assistedUnits</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>attributedOrders</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Orders (or transactions) attributed to a click or impression</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>ctr</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Click-through-rate (CTR), calculated by dividing <code>clicks</code> by <code>productImpressions</code> for Product and Product Category report types; for all other report types, the appropriate <code>placementImpressions</code> is used instead</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>cpc</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Cost-per-click (CPC), calculated by dividing <code>spend</code> by <code>clicks</code>; inclusive of platform fees</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>roas</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Return-on-ad-spend (ROAS), calculated by dividing <code>attributedSales</code> by <code>spend</code>; inclusive of platform fees</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>cpo</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Cost-per-order (CPO), calculated by dividing<code>spend</code> by <code>attributedOrders</code>; inclusive of platform fees</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>assistedSales</code></td>
      <td>Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Sales revenue attributed to a click or impression on the path to purchase leading to the sale, exclusive of <code>attributedSales</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>assistedUnits</code></td>
      <td>Line-item</td>
      <td>summary, pageType, keyword, product category, product</td>
      <td>Units sold attributed to a click or impression on the path to purchase leading to the sale; <code>attributedUnits</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>uniqueVisitors</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary</td>
      <td>Number of unique visitors an impression painted for</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td><code>frequency</code></td>
      <td>Campaign, Line-item</td>
      <td>summary</td>
      <td>An average representing how often an impression has been painted for the same user</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

## **Attributed Transactions Report Dimensions & Metrics**

* Note this report contains historical data, so you can always determine how each sale was attributed according to the data and rules enforced at the time.

* Keep this in mind when comparing data against other reports - properties such as `advProductCategory` could drift as product categories may evolve.

* All columns are available for both the **campaign** and **line-item** levels

* Learn more about our Attribution Rules in [CMax Help Center](https://help.retailmedia.criteo.com/kb/guide/en/about-attribution-sZH1iCyq5L/Steps/1034909)
