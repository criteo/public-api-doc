This article will help you to access your Criteo developer account. This will give you access to Developer Portal, as well as to the other Criteo platforms.

1. Go to our landing page at our [Criteo Developer Portal](https://developers.criteo.com/) and click on Get Started or go directly to our [Criteo Developer Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/)

2. Login with your existing Criteo account or contact your Criteo representative to create an account.

3. Once logged in, you will be redirected to the [Criteo Developer Dashboard](https://partners.criteo.com/). Please click on the Developer Portal link on your account screen if the redirection fails.

If you want to modify your account, please see our article [Managing my developer account](/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/managing-my-developer-account)
