An audience represents **all users who interacted with specific brands or categories over a defined period**, and can be specifically targeted for your campaigns.

<Info>
  In Preview, we offer four sets of endpoints:

  * [Audiences](/retail-media/v2025-preview/docs/audiences-endpoints)
  * [Audience segments](/retail-media/v2025-preview/docs/audience-segments)
  * [Audiences size](/retail-media/v2025-preview/docs/audience-size)
  * [Audience-segments size](/retail-media/v2025-preview/docs/audience-segments-size)
</Info>

<Note>
  **Commerce Max Help Center**

  You can find more information about how to build  audiences and audience segments in Criteo C-Max Help Center, on [this page](https://help.retailmedia.criteo.com/kb/guide/en/offsite-build-an-audience-bxFM6LtmvS/Steps/2973790,3269127) for Offsite, and [this page](https://help.retailmedia.criteo.com/kb/guide/en/onsite-display-build-an-audience-5a0BoXiXmP/Steps/2360680) for Onsite Display.
</Note>
