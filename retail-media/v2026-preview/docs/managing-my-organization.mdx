This article will explain to you how to edit your organization's information.

1. Click on the top left corner menu and select settings

![1650](https://files.readme.io/9e4c227-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201032.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201032.png")

2. Click on the pencil logo to Edit your current Organization information and save your changes

![1873](https://files.readme.io/41a0a18-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201104.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201104.png")

3. If you want to change another Organization's information\
   a. Go back to the My Apps screen\
   b. Select the Organization in the Organization screen accessible at the top right corner

![461](https://files.readme.io/52272fd-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201338.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201338.png")

c.     Change the Organization

![1425](https://files.readme.io/9f12117-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201418.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 201418.png")

d.	Follow steps 1 and 2

If you have any question about required fields please see the [Create your organization](/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/create-your-organization)  guide.
