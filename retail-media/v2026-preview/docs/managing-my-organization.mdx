This article will explain to you how to edit your organization's information.

1. Click on the top left corner menu and select settings

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/9e4c227-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201032.png" />
</Frame>

2. Click on the pencil logo to Edit your current Organization information and save your changes

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/41a0a18-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201104.png" />
</Frame>

3. If you want to change another Organization's information

   1. Go back to the My Apps screen
   2. Select the Organization in the Organization screen accessible at the top right corner

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/52272fd-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201338.png" />
      </Frame>

   3. Change the Organization

      <Frame>
        <img src="/images/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/9f12117-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_201418.png" />
      </Frame>

   4. Follow steps 1 and 2

If you have any question about required fields please see the [Create your organization](/retail-media/v2026-preview/docs/create-your-organization)  guide.
