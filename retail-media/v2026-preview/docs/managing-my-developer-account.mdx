This section will explain to you how to edit your account information.

Click the top right corner to access your Criteo Account space.

![332](https://files.readme.io/d641ca8-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_200313.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 200313.png")

Edit your Name, Last Name, and Password (to change your password, you will need to provide your current password to confirm your identity)

![1862](https://files.readme.io/3647d83-Capture_dcran_2020-10-02_200650.png "Capture d’écran 2020-10-02 200650.png")

Do you want to delete your Criteo account because you no longer work with Criteo? Please send an email to [support@criteo.com](mailto:support@criteo.com).
