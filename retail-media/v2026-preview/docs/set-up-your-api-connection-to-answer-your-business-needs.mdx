Next, you’ll want to define your API's scope and which domains your app needs to access. This ensures your app can connect to the right endpoints, split into two steps.

Choose the Criteo product you’d like to build with: Criteo Marketing Solutions (Commerce Growth) or Criteo Retail Media (i.e., select a Service). Click on Save to access the Authorization section. 

<Warning>
  **🚨 NOTE**

  Once you click on save, you won't be able to switch between Marketing Solutions, Publisher, or Retail Media anymore! So please chose carefully (and in case you made a mistake, it is not that bad; create a new app 😉
</Warning>

From there, you can select which Authorization to access. You need to specify whether you need “read-only” or “manage” access to your end users’ accounts. For example, you may want “read-only” to retrieve a report or “manage” permissions so you can add, remove, or list products online items

💡 You can update the Authorizations as many times as you want before validating your app through the Generate consent link button
